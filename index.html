About Us

Join the club! We are a passionate and an enthusiastic club all about having fun on and off the mountain.

Summit Springs offers trips to local resorts for skiers and snowboarders who want the fun, companionship and convenience of group travel. We also provide vacation trips to destination resorts around the world. As a group, we can bargain with travel agents to keep costs reasonable.

Located in Seattle, Summit Springs is run by an executive team of passionate snow sport members that come together and organize trips and events throughout the year.
